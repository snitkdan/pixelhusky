Pixel Husky

Pixel Husky is the first outward facing design club at the University of Washington open to all departments and majors. Our mission is to build a community for designers, and non-designers who are interested in design and prospective designers. Pixel Husky is where these groups can interact and inspire a love for design.

Talks

Hear professionals open up about life within the industry and work out in the field

Panels

Get the chance to be the interviewer and get answers to questions you want to know

Workshops

Connect with others, work in teams and learn about design with hands-on experiences

Community

We offer a space for designers, non-designers interested in design and perspective designers all sit together and build relationships

Upcoming Events

Here is a full schedule for our events this fall! Message us on Facebook or talk to us if you want to learn about what we are doing for winter :)

October 7 – Design Majors Showcase – McMahon Hall Pompeii Room // 3PM - 5PM // Friday

This showcase is meant to highlight the similarities and differences between the various Design related departments within the UW. Upperclassmen from each department will be presenting their work and answering general questions. Come enjoy an evening exploring Design at the UW!

October 12 – General Meeting + Marshmallow Challenge // 5:30PM - 7PM // Wednesday

Meet your 2016-17 officers, learn about the club, and get a sneak peek of our future events. We also have a sweet group design challenge prepared so get yourself ready to build your fluffy dreams into a puffy soft reality (marshmallows...you’re working with marshmallows) ;) Winners for the design challenge will receive mystery prizes from Pixel Husky! See you then!

October 29 – Design Internship Panel // 5:30PM - 7PM // Thursday

We invited a group of panelists from various design-related majors to share their unique stories with you and answer all your questions and concerns. Important questions include how to find an internship, how to choose among multiple offers, what to look for in a great internship etc. Come and join us as we dive into the internship experiences of other fellow huskies just like you and help guide you to your very own internship opportunity!

November 9 – Designer Scavenger Hunt // 5:30PM - 7PM // Wednesday

Team up with fellow Huskies and gather clues to pull together information about the design process, design related majors, and resources offered to design students. Under the guidance of one of our sketchbook wielding, Indiana Jones shaming design professors, teams will navigate the purple and gold campus, emerging with either the crowd singing their glory, or with encyclopedic knowledge of the design world at UW. Both prospects are something that you don’t want to miss out on.

November 17 – Presentation 101 // 5:30PM - 7PM // Thursday

This event is still in the process of evolving. Check back anytime for updates :)
And I assure you this event is going to be super helpful!
